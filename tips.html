General Privacy Tips

Use Strong, Unique Passwords

Employ a password manager to create and store complex passwords for each account.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security by requiring a second form of verification.

Review Privacy Settings

Regularly check and adjust settings on social media and apps to limit data sharing.

Be Cautious with Links and Downloads

Avoid clicking suspicious links or downloading unknown files to prevent malware.

Limit Location Sharing

Turn off location services when not needed to protect your whereabouts.

Use VPNs for Public Wi-Fi

Protect your data on unsecured networks with a virtual private network.
